Frequently Asked Questions

What is DermaScan and how does it work?
DermaScan is an AI-powered platform that helps you analyze and detect potential skin diseases through image scans. Our technology uses advanced algorithms to provide preliminary insights but does not replace professional medical diagnosis.
Is the AI scan accurate?
While our AI is trained on a large dataset to provide accurate predictions, it is not 100% foolproof. Always consult a certified dermatologist for a definitive diagnosis.
How do I perform a skin scan?
Simply upload a clear photo of the affected skin area under good lighting conditions. Avoid makeup or filters to ensure the best analysis results.
How is my personal and medical information protected?
We take your privacy seriously. All your data is encrypted and stored securely, and we do not share your information with third parties without your consent.
Can DermaScan replace a doctor?
No. DermaScan is a tool to assist and inform you but cannot replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
What should I do if I get a concerning result?
If DermaScan flags a potential issue, please consult a dermatologist promptly for further evaluation.
How do I update my medical information?
You can update your profile and medical information anytime from the Medical Info page after signing in.
Who can use DermaScan?
DermaScan is designed for individuals aged 18 and above who want to monitor their skin health conveniently using AI technology.
What if I face technical issues with the app?
For technical support, please contact our support team via the Contact Us section or email support@dermascan.com.
